I create a column called steps_noNA that has the average steps for an interval when the steps for that day aren't actually known

activity2 <- activity %>% 
  left_join(Ave_Steps, by = 'interval') %>% 
  mutate(steps_noNA = ifelse(is.na(steps), Ave_Steps, steps))
